Purpose of Course

The purpose of this course is to provide an understanding of the scope of cybercrime and its potential impact on the organisation and individual. We require that all of our employees recognise the risk of cybercrime in their work-based activities. The couse is therefore mandatory for all employees and is an essential part of employee induction.

The benefits to the organisation from such learning include:

  • Minimising the financial losses from cybercrime and the cost of recovering from it
  • Preventing in productivy and diminished staff morale
    - both of which can result from cyber attacks
  • Avoiding reputational damage with customers, service users investors and other stakeholders
